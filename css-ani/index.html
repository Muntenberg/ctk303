GNU / Linux

In the realm of code where choices unfold,

GNU/Linux shines, a story told.

Open and free, like nature's breeze,

Source code dances, through the trees.

Yet, in the land of Windows closed,

A tale of limits, it supposed.

Proprietary chains, a captive mind,

Freedom's melody, hard to find.

Behind sealed windows, a walled garden stands,

Where control resides in corporate hands.

Closed source whispers, secrets untold,

In this realm of limits, a story unfolds.

But beware the affliction, the malware's kin,

Proprietary software, a captive within.

In the world of bytes, where freedom is key,

Closed code, a malady that hinders the free.

So let us celebrate the open way,

Where collaboration leads the day.

In the fight against this digital plight,

Eradicate the malaise, let freedom take flight.

As we traverse the digital domain,

Apple's walls remind us, a closed terrain.

Yet, in the dance of code and the songs it brings,

GNU/Linux and Stallman's vision, our hearts shall sing.
